## Walkthrough 1: Public Website

1. Open the home page.
2. Browse featured room types.
3. Go to rooms listing and apply availability filters.
4. Open a room details page and create a reservation after login.

## Walkthrough 2: Guest Experience

1. Login as `guest1@example.com`.
2. Open `Guest Dashboard`.
3. Review reservations and invoices.
4. Open `Service Requests` and submit a request.
5. Open `Feedback & Ratings` and inspect checked-out stay feedback.
6. Open `Notifications` and mark items as read.
7. Open `My Profile` and update guest details.

## Walkthrough 3: Reception Operations

1. Login as `reception@luxurystay.com`.
2. Open `Check-in Desk`.
3. Load live room availability for a confirmed reservation and assign a room if needed.
4. Complete check-in with ID details.
5. Open `Check-out Desk`.
6. Add folio charges, generate/finalize invoice, collect payment, and complete checkout.
7. Open `Billing` to review invoices and payments.

## Walkthrough 4: Housekeeping Operations

1. Login as `housekeeping@luxurystay.com`.
2. Open `Assigned Tasks`.
3. Start and complete a cleaning task.
4. Open `Room Cleaning Board`.
5. Report a room-linked maintenance issue.

## Walkthrough 5: Maintenance Operations

1. Login as `maintenance@luxurystay.com`.
2. Open `Open Requests`.
3. Assign an issue to yourself.
4. Add resolution notes and resolve it.
5. Open `Closed Requests History` and close the resolved issue.

## Walkthrough 6: Admin Oversight

1. Login as `admin@luxurystay.com`.
2. Review `Admin Dashboard` charts and cards.
3. Open `Staff`, `Rooms`, and `Reservations`.
4. Open `Reports & Analytics` and export a CSV report.
5. Open `Hotel Settings` and update a property field.
6. Open `Notifications` and `Audit Logs`.

## Walkthrough 7: Manager Insights

1. Login as `manager@luxurystay.com`.
2. Review `Manager Dashboard`.
3. Open `Occupancy Overview`.
4. Open `Revenue Reports`.
5. Open `Notifications`.

## Demo Notes

- Seed data includes rooms in available, occupied, cleaning, reserved, and maintenance states.
- Seed data includes pending, confirmed, checked-in, checked-out, and cancelled reservations.
- Billing data includes unpaid, partially paid, and paid invoice examples.
- Housekeeping, maintenance, feedback, service requests, and notifications are pre-seeded for live demonstration.
